GEOG0013: Geography in the Field 1 (GIF1) 2022/23

Part 2: Information about Workshop 2

Welcome to GIF1’s Introduction to GIS. A few weeks ago, you were introduced to the basic statistical methods for exploring & representing secondary data from Barcelona, using a programming software tool known as R/RStudio. This session focuses on the themes of GIS (i.e., Geographical Information Systems). Here, we will provide you with an introduction on how to visual geographic data collected from the field trip in Barcelona using ArcGIS Map, as well as harnessing the ArcGIS StoryMaps as a platform to digital storytelling with maps.

Self-guided tutorials

Prior to the workshops which will be supported by the teaching assistants (PGTAs), you will have the opportunity to go through the self-guided tutorials presented on this website on your own. The teaching materials have been designed to be accessible to anyone who has never used any GIS software including ArcGIS Maps or ArcGIS Storymaps. The tutorials will be very prescriptive with instructions, and these instructions will be support by guided videos.

The whole point of these self-guided tutorials are to prepare you for the group workshop which will be facilitated by the PGTAs, so you can use the data collected from Barcelona to make you own GIS atlas and storymap. Note that this exercise is part of the assessment tied to the submission of your diaries. Details on this will come shortly.
